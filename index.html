Top 3 places to visit in Kolkata

Visit Biswa Bangla Gate

Kolkata Gate is an arch-monument in the city of New Town, Kolkata, West Bengal, India.

Dakshineswar Temple

Dakshineswar Kali Temple is a Hindu navaratna temple in Dakshineswar, Kolkata, West Bengal, India.

Victoria Memorial

The Victoria Memorial is a large marble building on the Maidan in Central Kolkata, having its entrance on the Queen's Way.
